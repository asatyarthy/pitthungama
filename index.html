Pitthungama - 7th Annual Diwali Mela

4 Different Competitions

Please send your entries by 31st Aug 2023

1. Drawing Competition

(participation fee is $7 per person)

3 categories that you can participate in:

* 3-8 years - No Theme
* 9-13 years - Animals
* 14-18 years - Indian Monument

Rules

- Paper will be provided (11x14inches) please bring your own pencil and eraser
- Time: 45 mins
- Competition will start promptly at 12:30 pm and the winner will be announced at 4pm
- You can use water colors or acrylic or crayons (you will need to bring what you want to use)
- If you want to use canvas you will need to bring that.

2. Dance Competition

(participation fee is $30 per group/$10 per person)

Two age groups:

* Kids - 2 to 17 years
* Adults - 18 to 99 years

3. Rangoli Making Competition

(participation fee is $25 per group/ $15 per person)

Rules

- Board will be provided and Rangoli art should not exceed that space
- Time: 45-60 mins
- Rangoli competition will start promptly at 11:30 pm and the winner will be announced at 4pm
- All required decoration material, white rangoli and colors needs to be carried by the participant

4. Dessert Making Competition

(participation fee is $7 per person)

Rules

- Only homemade Deserts ONLY in the contest, Store bought will be disqualified and eaten immediately!!
- Bring in about 7-10 pieces
- Judges Decision will be final
- Sugar Free Deserts accepted
- Item will be judged based on taste and presentation
- Drop off your desert between 12-12:30 pm and the winner will be announced at 4pm
